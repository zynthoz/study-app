# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students, self-directed learners, and academic achievers preparing for exams, reviews, or mastering new domains. Their context is active study sessions where focus, organization, and efficient learning are paramount.

## Product Purpose
StudyForge acts as an AI-powered study companion. It parses uploaded textbooks/materials (documents or images), synthesizes high-quality markdown notes via Gemini 1.5 Pro, creates configurable practice exams, scores user performance, and organizes all resources (notes, exams, files) neatly inside designated subject folders.

## Brand Personality
- Focused: Minimalist layout that directs cognitive energy to study materials.
- Premium: Sleek, dark mode aesthetics using Outifit/Inter typography, subtle glassmorphic textures, and elegant fluid gradients.
- Intelligent: Polished, responsive, and responsive micro-interactions that feel modern and helper-like.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, dated school portal dashboards (e.g., Canvas/Moodle) with dense lists and confusing nested routes.
- Bland white-label administrative tables.
- Typical 2026 AI-wrapper boilerplate: overly rounded borders (32px+), saturated cream/sand backgrounds, side-stripe cards, and generic SaaS eyebrows.

## Design Principles
- Readability First: Long-form synthesized notes and exam questions must maintain comfortable line lengths (65-75ch) and excellent text contrast.
- Focus and Flow: Exam-taking sessions should feel completely immersive and distraction-free.
- Single-hub Cohesion: Keep subjects, notes, and exams tightly unified in a master-detail dashboard, preventing jarring page-to-page navigation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast targeting WCAG AA compliance (text contrast >= 4.5:1 against dark backgrounds).
- Clean typographic hierarchy with readable letter-spacing.
- Reduced motion alternatives for fluid floating background animations.
